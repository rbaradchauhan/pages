Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Business Card Scanner mobile application ("Application") developed by Pramaasoft ("Service Provider"). The Application allows users to scan business cards, extract information, and manage contacts digitally.

Information Collection and Use

The Application may collect the following information:

  • Photos captured using your device camera for scanning business cards
  • Text information extracted from scanned cards (name, phone, email, company, etc.)
  • Login details when you sign in using Google or Yahoo authentication
  • Basic device information such as device type and operating system
  • Application usage data for improving performance and stability

Business card images are processed to extract information and populate contact fields.

Data Storage

  • If you are not logged in, scanned data is stored locally on your device only.
  • If you log in using Google or Yahoo, your scanned card data may be securely stored and synced using Firebase services.
  • You have control over your stored data within the application.

Camera Access

The Application requires camera access solely for capturing business card images. No images are captured without your explicit action.

Third-Party Services

The Application may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore / Database
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Sign-In
  • Yahoo Authentication

These services have their own privacy policies regarding data handling.

Data Sharing

The Service Provider does not sell or rent personal data. Data may be shared only:

  • To provide authentication and cloud sync services
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect user safety or prevent fraud

Data Retention

User data remains stored:

  • Locally on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • In Firebase cloud storage while your account remains active
